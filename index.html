Padang Kasna Temukus

A Garden full of edelweiss flower combined with beautiful view of agung mountain.

About


Created by Local People


Many temukus villagers have cultivation Kasna flowers, villagers do this because kesna flower only can grow in temukus villages or more easily at foot of mount agung. usually temukus villagers use kesna flower in Hindu religious ceremonies like galungan and kuningan at once as economic driver for temukus villagers. For local people kesna flower is symbol of immortality by having color like snow and looks beautiful.



Location

Desa Temukus Pempatan, Kecamatan Rendang, Kabupaten Karangasem, Bali 80863 Open Maps



Attractions



Beautiful View

Beautiful View of Agung Mountain and arrangement of edelweiss flower.

The weather in Temukus Village is really cool, as far as the eye can see there are only hills and flower gardens. Besides Padang Kasna, don't miss also to see the gemitir flower garden in Temukus Village
